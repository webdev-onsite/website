On Site Solutions are specialists in placing developers On Site working directly with clients to satisfy their legacy and bespoke applications development and support requirements.

Mission critical legacy applications maintenance and development is a major component of our work which allows clients to continue to gain value and fully utilise and exploit applications which have been central to organisations functions for, in many cases, decades.

Bespoke applications developments are another major area of our operations. We have designed, developed and continue to support mission critical applications for our clients.

We actively strive to improve delivery of our services by optimising existing techniques and technologies along with the adoption of new advances and approaches.

We support our client’s application needs depending on their specific platforms and requirements.

We develop desktop, intranet and terminal based applications in both Windows and Linux operating systems environments.

Bespoke applications developments are another major area of our operations. We have designed, developed and continue to support mission critical applications for clients operating at a national and regional levels.
